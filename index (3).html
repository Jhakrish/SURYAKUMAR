Suryakumar Ashok Yadav (born 14 September 1990) is an Indian international cricketer who plays for the Indian cricket team. He plays as a right handed batsman for Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League and for the Mumbai cricket team in Indian domestic cricket. He is regarded as one of the best T20I cricketers in modern cricket.
